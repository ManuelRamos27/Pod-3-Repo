Here's some info about something I like

The tres leches cake at Churrasco's is a paradox; it is both rich and light at the same time. The cake is coarse-textured yellow cake, a little bit dense. Three types of milk are poured over the cake until it is saturated in dairy-liciousness. Topping it off is the smoothest, most delicious Swiss meringue I've ever had. Try it, you will not regret the calories or the expense! There's a link below to Churrasco's website where you can make a reservation for dinner.
